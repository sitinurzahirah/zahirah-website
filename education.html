A journey as a student



Here are a story of my journey as a student starting from primary school, secondary school and university.

Nelson Mandela said: Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.



PRIMARY SCHOOL



Sekolah Kebangsaan Kubang Kerian 3 was my primary school. I entered this school during standard 5
as before this my school at Sekolah Kebangsaan Bukit Jawa 1 as our family are moved out. Managed to score
big examination in primary school with getting 5A's in UPSR 2010.This picture was taken during result
UPSR ceremony and one of my best memories in middle school getting straight A.



SECONDARY SCHOOL



Entering secondary school in Maahad Pengajian Islam at Kota Bharu. Being to this school at Form 4 as to enter
the Science Stream after getting 5A's in Penilaian Menangah Rendah. Be a student in Science Stream for me
a quite challenging as a lot of formulas, terms to understand. Giving all of effort to big examination in high school which is
Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia then got 2A's. After all I am still feels so blessed for the result even not getting straight A.



UNIVERSITY



Finished my diploma journey in Universiti Teknologi Mara Kelantan Kampus Machang, Diploma in
Office Management and Technology. My diploma journey was so wonderful for me as I got to experienced in getting a dean list and managed to get 4 flat in final year. I still feels so surreal to me on getting those happy journey in study.




Now, I am currently further study in Degree in Bachelor in Office Systems Management (HONs) also in same University. I am hoping for my degree journey to be smooth and grad on time.
